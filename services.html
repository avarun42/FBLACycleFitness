Seminars

Cycle Fitness holds a variety of seminars geared towards helping our customers make the most out of their bicycles.

Bike Safety

Biking can be quite dangerous if the proper precautions are not taken and in this seminar we'll teach you how to have fun while staying safe.

Cycling for Fitness

Biking can be an excellent source of cardiovascular exercise. In this seminar we'll teach you all the tips and tricks you need to use cycling to lose weight and stay fit!

Choosing the right bike

With the many different options we offer, choosing a bike can be an intimidating process. In this seminar, we'll assess your wants and needs to pick the perfect bike for you.

Maintaining your Bike

A good bike can last you the rest of your life, if taken care of properly. By buying your bicycle from Cycle Fitness, you're already getting an outstanding bike. With this seminar, we'll teach you how to take care of your bike so it'll last you a lifetime.

Fixing your Bike

We get it. Bikes break. Chains come off, Pedals get worn, it happens. With this seminar, we'll teach you how to fix any mishaps that might happen with your cycle.

Competitive Cycling

Cycling can be an extremely entertaining and rewarding sport. Join this seminar to learn more about the world of competitive cycling. It doesn't matter if you plan on being an athlete or a spectator!
