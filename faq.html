FAQ

Below are some frequently asked questions. They may answer some of your concerns or questions.

  • Home
  • FAQ
  • 1

    What if I don't know what kind of bike I need?

    If you are unsure about what kind of bike you want, come to one our seminar events where we discuss the different types of bikes and the different needs they fulfill. Or, you can stop by our shop and one of our sales representatives will be more than happy to assist you in your decision. Getting the right bike is incredibly important to your biking experience and it is our mission to provide you with your perfect bike. You can also contact us through our form with your specific requests and we'll be happy to reccomend a bike to you.

  • 2

    How can I learn more about my bike?

    If you'd like to learn more about the world of cycling, feel free to stop by any one of our many seminars. We offer lessons on topics ranging from competitive cycling to how to repair your bicycle. All of these seminars are taught by experienced bikers and you'll be able to learn a great deal from them.

  • 3

    Do you only sell bikes?

    Not at all! We sell a great deal of biking equipment and gear, and we also service bikes. However, in order to get these extras, you'll have to stop by our store in Manhattan. We'll be happy to assist you in finding the best gear you need to have an amazing biking experience!
