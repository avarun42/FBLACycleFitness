Who we are

Cycle Fitness is a business with a mission, to give everyone access to the best bicycle in the world for an affordable price. We began our journey after we realized how inefficient the current Cycle Market was and we decided we needed to change that. Since we're all avid bikers, we know exactly what every biker wants in his cycle. From the dilettantes to the connoisseurs, Cycle Fitness will meet all your biking needs.
